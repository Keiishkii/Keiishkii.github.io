    I am currently employed as a Research Assistant within the MINE Cluster at Bournemouth University, my work typically involves the development of solutions for experiments or research projects.

  • EmBodied N170:

    EmBodied-N170 is a project currently running within the MINE research cluster, the projects focus is to record and analyse a subjects' perception of facial stimuli from within a VR setting.

    My role on this project was to develop the experiment within Unity, this required modeling and building the scene's environment, programming the experiments game-flow and the transmission and collection of experiment data between multiple devices on a local network.

    BAB Football is another project I am currently working on. The project involved the implementation of pre-recorded live game data of real football matches and replaying the game within a virtual environment, with the aid of VR.
