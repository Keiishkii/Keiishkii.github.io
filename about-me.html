About Me and Rianna and Paul Brown

My name is Charlie Lloyd-Buckingham, I have worked as a game's programmer since 2020.

When working on a game, either as a product or research tool, my work can span from the creation of simple mechanics to complex systems, these could occur as features within pre-existing products or the development of something brand new. My skills involve: programming in C#, C++, Java, CSS, HTML, JavaScript, and Python; asset creation for textures, 3D-Models and animation; creating solutions for complex systems; a strong understanding of the mathematics required for game development; and the integration of unique input modalities within game engines like Unity, such as VR, EEG, EMG and custom electronics.
